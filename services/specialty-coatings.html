J&H Painting provides professional specialty coating and finishing services for commercial, residential, and new construction projects across Florida and nationwide. Our team is experienced in applying high-performance coating systems designed for durability, protection, and long-term performance in demanding environments.

We utilize professional spray equipment, proper surface preparation, and manufacturer-recommended application methods to ensure consistent coverage and high-quality results on every project.

Scope Includes:

  • Direct-to-Metal (DTM) coatings for doors, frames, and metal surfaces
  • Elastomeric and protective coating systems
  • High-durability commercial coatings
  • Spray-applied coatings for large-scale surfaces
  • Specialty primers and sealers
  • High-performance interior and exterior finishes
  • Surface-specific coating applications
  • Touch-ups, refinishing, and protective recoating
