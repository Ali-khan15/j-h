Proper Surface Prep for Durability

Proper preparation is essential for long-term exterior performance. J&H Painting follows a structured preparation process that may include pressure cleaning, surface repairs, sanding, caulking, priming, and coating application to ensure proper adhesion and a durable finish suitable for exterior conditions.
