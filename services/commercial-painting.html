J&H Painting provides professional commercial painting services for large-scale facilities, new construction projects, tenant build-outs, and repaints across Florida and nationwide. Our experienced crews, structured project coordination, and scalable workforce allow us to execute projects efficiently while maintaining consistent quality, safety, and dependable scheduling.

We work closely with general contractors, project managers, and facility teams to ensure all work is completed in accordance with project specifications, site requirements, and construction timelines.

Scope Includes:

  • Interior & exterior commercial painting for large-scale projects
  • Airless spray applications and high-production coating systems
  • Lift and high-access area painting (ceilings, exteriors, and large structures)
  • Comprehensive surface preparation including patching, sanding, caulking, and priming
  • Tenant improvements, build-outs, and occupied facility coordination
  • Retail, office, educational, and institutional facilities
  • Phased project execution aligned with construction schedules and inspections
  • Repaints, touch-ups, and detailed punch list completion
