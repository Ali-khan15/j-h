Proper Surface Prep for Adhesion

Proper surface preparation is essential for successful wallcovering installation. J&H Painting ensures surfaces are clean, smooth, primed, and properly conditioned to support adhesion and long-term durability. Our preparation process includes patching imperfections, sanding surfaces, and priming substrates to meet manufacturer and project requirements.
