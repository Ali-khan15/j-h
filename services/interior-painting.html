J&H Painting provides professional interior painting services for residential, commercial, and new construction properties across Florida and nationwide. Our team delivers clean, precise finishes while maintaining organized work areas, proper protection, and efficient project coordination in both occupied spaces and active construction environments.

We work closely with homeowners, builders, property managers, and general contractors to ensure consistent quality, reliable scheduling, and professional execution on every interior project.

Scope Includes:

  • Interior wall, ceiling, and trim painting
  • New construction interior painting
  • Multi-unit and production builder projects
  • Tenant build-outs and interior renovations
  • High-production spray applications and fine finish work
  • Surface preparation including patching, sanding, caulking, and priming
  • Touch-ups, repaints, and final punch list completion
  • Coordination with superintendents, project managers, and site schedules
